 - Terms, data, values.
 - Dos and Don'ts
   - Don't commit or flush after every modification, unless you really need
     to.  Modifications are much much faster if they're grouped together.  If
     you must flush every modification immediately, consider using a small
     database of recent modifications, merged with a larger database of
     earlier modifications.
